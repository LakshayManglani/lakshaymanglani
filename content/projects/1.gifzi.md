## The Problem

**In the existing product:**

- Branding and visual identity lacked consistency
- Systems (Coins, Plus) felt disconnected
- Slow, interruptive experience
- Too many steps to purchase
- Hard to find gift cards

**A “quick gift” didn’t feel quick.**

---

## The Breakthrough

> I didn’t just redesign screens — I redesigned the system.

I led a complete redesign of the platform — covering UI, UX flows, branding, and
system consistency.

![Gifzi Branding Before & After](/gifzi/gifzi-branding-before-after.png)

---

## 3 Decisions That Changed Everything

### 1. Search > Browsing

![Search UI](/gifzi/gifzi-search-bar-before-after.png)

Users don’t explore — they _know what they want_.  
Search became the primary action.

---

### 2. Merge the System

![Gifzi Checkout Screen](/gifzi/gifzi-checkout-screen.png)

Gifzi Plus + Coins were fragmented.  
I unified them into **one seamless layer** inside checkout.

---

### 3. Faster Decisions

![Product Page Before vs After](/gifzi/gifzi-product-screen-before-after.png)

The original flow slowed users down with a single path (Add to Cart).

I introduced:

- **Buy Now** for instant purchase
- **Related products** for quick discovery

Reducing friction at the moment of decision.
